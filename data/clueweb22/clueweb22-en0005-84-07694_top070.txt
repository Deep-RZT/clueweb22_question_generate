Accessories to Bring Your Smoking Experience to a New Level
You crave a cigarette after a good meal. You enjoy having a cigar on special occasions. Whether you’re a regular smoker or only smoke once in a while, WeeWorld is the place to go. Each product is crafted using high-quality materials that are safe to use, making your experiences more flavorful and enjoyable. From humidor solutions and humidifiers, to cigarette filters and organic leaf rolls, we have the accessories you need to ensure your smoking experience is a pleasurable one.
Less Tar, Same Great Taste
If you enjoy smoking but are concerned about the hazards, WeeWorld’s Disposable Cigarette Filters may be the solution you’re looking for. These filters help to reduce the tar by up to 90% and substantially lowers the nicotine level, keeping a good majority of harmful substances from entering your lungs. To top it off, it doesn’t alter the taste or flavor of the cigarette or take anything away from your smoking experience.
Reusable Filters
Each filter is made of high-quality, non-toxic plastic for safe use. Simply insert your cigarette into the filter and it’s ready for use. The clear plastic allows you to visibly see how much tar is trapped in the filter as it turns darker and darker after each cigarette. Depending on the type of cigarettes, the amount of tar present and the length of your drags, these easy-to-use, disposable filters can last up to six uses.
Filter out Harmful Substances with Disposable Cigarettes Filters
Feel Better
By reducing the level of tar, nicotine and other harmful substances from entering the lungs, these Disposable Cigarette Filters from WeeWorld will help to reduce coughing and shortness of breath, as well as relieve sore throat in a few short weeks. Best of all, they will help to reduce the number of cigarettes you smoke and can aid in helping you to quit smoking once and for all.
Get Rid of Stains
Embarrassed by the yellow stains on your fingers and discolored teeth every time you smile? While the most important benefit is reducing tar intake to your lungs, you’ll be happy to know that these Disposable Cigarette Filters also minimize yellow-brown stains on your teeth and fingers. Now you can get rid of nicotine stains on your fingers and regain the natural color of your teeth too.
Large Quantity
WeeWorld’s Disposable Cigarette Filters come in a resealable container of 300 disposable filters. Each one measures 1.18 inches in length, easily fitting on the end of your cigarette filter to trap most of the tar and nicotine without compromising the flavor. The black substance is gradually collected after each cigarette and once it is full, simply replace with a new filter.
