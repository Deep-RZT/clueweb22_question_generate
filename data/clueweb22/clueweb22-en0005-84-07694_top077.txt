Product Description
The Cigarette Carton Plastic Container is designed to store and protect your freshly rolled cigarettes. It's heavy gauge construction and tight seal keeps your smokes safe and fresh. It has room to add a small humidifier such as a Humidipak or a Water Pillow, if you desire to increase the humidity level of the cigarettes. The Cigarette Carton Plastic Container can also hold a full box of 200 count king sized tubes. The Clear Lid is made of .020" thick PET and measures 11"L x 4 3/4"W x 3/8"T. The Translucent Blue Base is made of .040" thick PET and measures 10"L x 4 3/8"W x 2 3/4"T. The plastic container ships empty. Cigarettes & tubes shown are sold separately. Made in the USA.
