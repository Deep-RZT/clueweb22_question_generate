Clubhouse
Doña Julia Golf Club, has a Clubhouse equipe to covers all golfers needs and visitors
Doña Julia Golf Club, has a Clubhouse equipe to covers all golfers needs and visitors, like changing rooms for gents and ladies, restaurant & bar, Pro – Shop, Caddie Master and offices.
At the restaurant, you can enjoy  a refreshment or even a snack before or after your game in our magnificent golf course, or just simply visit us, and taste some of our dishes that our chef offers you every day.
