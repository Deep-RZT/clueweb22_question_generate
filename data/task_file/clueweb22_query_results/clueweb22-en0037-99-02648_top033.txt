Strategically located 250m from the beach, Azata Delmare is a complex of 74 apartments of 2 and 3 bedrooms, on three levels, divided on 7 residential buildings and located in a gated residential community, which provides an important privacy and security for the neighborhood. The complex has a beautiful infinity pool, a co-working area and an indoor gym. The orientation, mostly to the south, gives brightness to all the apartments.
Designed with an avant-garde and contemporary style. The apartments and penthouses have been conceived to enjoy sea and mountain views, with functional and modern layouts and excellent construction qualities focused on efficiency and energy savings and respectful with the environment. Spacious terraces are projected from which you can enjoy beautiful sea and mountain views. parking and storage room included in the price.
