How to clean?
1. Unscrew black mouthpiece (twist counter-clock wise).
2. Once apart, clean both pieces using the needle tool and wipe with napkins
3. Using warm water or diluted alcohol to help cleaning out the sticky tar.
4. Dry out and make sure the tube is completely clear.
Note:
1. Please keep the product away from children
2. Avoid high temperatures, exposure to the Sun and immersion in water.
